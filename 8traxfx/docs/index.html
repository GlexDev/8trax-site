For Buyers

Buyers do NOT need the bot. Buyers browse the market channel and contact sellers through their pages.

Buyer Flow (Simple)

  1. Browse listings on the Marketplace Channel: t.me/EightTraxMarket
  2. Open the seller page from the listing (each seller has a dedicated page/profile)
  3. Read details (tool type, requirements, pricing, setup notes)
  4. Ask questions in the Discussion Room: t.me/EightTrax_Main/1
  5. Proceed with the seller based on the seller’s listed instructions

Tip: Always confirm tool version (MT4/MT5), symbol pairs, timeframe, and required settings before buying.

How Sellers List a Tool (Recommended Fields)

  • Tool Type: EA / Indicator / Setfile / Strategy
  • Platform: MT4 or MT5
  • Pairs: (e.g. XAUUSD, EURUSD…)
  • Timeframes: (e.g. M5, M15, H1…)
  • Requirements: broker type, minimum deposit, spread limits, VPS needed, etc.
  • Pricing: clear price + what buyer receives
  • Support: what support you offer (installation/help/updates)

Keep it honest. Clean listings build trust and drive repeat buyers.

Safety & Marketplace Rules

8TraxFX is designed to reduce chaos and make tool trading clearer on Telegram.

What 8TraxFX Helps With

  • Structured listings (not random sales messages)
  • Seller pages/profiles for visibility
  • A single public feed where buyers can browse tools
  • A dedicated discussion room for questions and support

Rules (Simple)

  • No fake results or misleading profit claims
  • No stolen tools / reselling without rights
  • No spamming the community room
  • Respect buyers and respond professionally
